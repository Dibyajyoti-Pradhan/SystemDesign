# Problem
Design a global video streaming platform where users can upload, watch, search, and interact with videos at massive scale — similar to YouTube.

## Functional requirements
- Upload video with chunked, resumable uploads and progress tracking
- Stream video with adaptive bitrate, seek support, and low startup latency
- Search videos by title, tags, and category; surface trending content
- Personalized video recommendations based on watch history
- Comments, likes, and channel subscriptions with notifications

## Scale & non-functional
- DAU: ~500M MAU (2B total registered users)
- Upload volume: 500 hours of video uploaded per minute
- Watch volume: ~1B hours/day; ~11.6M concurrent viewers at peak
- Video startup latency: <2s (p99)
- Search latency: <100ms (p99)
- Availability: 99.99%
- Storage growth: ~300 TB/day transcoded; ~110 PB/year
- Storage durability: 11 nines

## Out of scope
- Live streaming
- Monetization / ads serving
- Creator analytics dashboard
- DRM / content licensing enforcement
